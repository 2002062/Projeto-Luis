NOVA University of Lisbon - School of Social Sciences and Humanities (NOVA FCSH) is located at the heart of Lisbon, in two campi: Avenida de Berna and Campolide. In the first campus, you can attend bachelor and masters degrees; in the second, Phds and research units. Both are close to subway, gardens, services and cultural infrastructures, such as Culturgest and Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation.

There are 12 departments covering the social sciences, the arts and the humanities.

Fields of Study

The fields of study that we have now at FCSH are:

  • Anthropology Communication Sciences Musicology Political Studies Portuguese Studies Philosophy Geography and Regional Planning History Art History Modern Languages, Cultures, and Literatures Linguistics Sociology
